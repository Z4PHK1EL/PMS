Requirements for Sacraments & Services
General Requirements
  • Valid ID of the applicant or parents/guardians
  • Recent passport-size photo (white background)
  • Filled-out application form (available at the parish office)
Specific Requirements by Sacrament/Service

  • Birth Certificate (PSA / NSO authenticated)
  • Marriage Contract of parents (if applicable)
  • Names & addresses of godparents (at least one male & one female)
  • Godparents must be confirmed Catholics

  • Marriage License (valid within 120 days)
  • Canonical Interview Certificate
  • Pre-Cana Seminar Certificate
  • Baptismal & Confirmation certificates (recently issued)
  • Birth Certificates of both parties

  • Proof of ownership or permission from owner
  • Preferred date and time
  • Optional: small offering for the priest

For confession: No documents needed – just come during scheduled times.

For Mass Intention: Stipend offering and preferred date(s).

Contact the parish office for more specific requirements.

Please prepare all documents in advance. Requirements may vary slightly depending on the case.
